This chocolate cake is soft, rich, and perfect for any celebration. One bite and you'll fall in love!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients (flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder).
  2. Add eggs, milk, and butter.
  3. Stir until smooth.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
  5. Let it cool and enjoy! Yummy!
